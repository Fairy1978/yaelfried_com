What Does the Process Look Like?

01

First Introductory Session at a Special Price

The introductory session is a full working session where we begin the transformation. We enter a state of trance and work with the subconscious.Many people leave this session already experiencing a sense of relief, release, or significant change.

02

Why an introductory session at a special price?

This is your opportunity to experience this process and feel the transformation. It also allows me to assess whether I can help you in the best possible way, and together we'll decide how to move forward.

03

The Therapeutic Process

The process consists of 5 initial sessions, with each session lasting 60-90 minutes. Additional sessions can be added if needed. In each session, we enter a state of trance, access the subconscious, release additional layers, and deepen the transformation.Most people experience significant change within 5-8 sessions. The transformation is permanent and continues developing even months after the process ends.
