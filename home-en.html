Frequently Asked Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Trance Safe?

Yes, absolutely. Trance is a natural state you experience every day, when you're so absorbed in a book that you don't hear anything around you, when you're gazing at a sunset and time disappears, when you're working on something and hours pass without you noticing, or when you're listening to music and completely get lost in the sound. You remain in complete control, hear every word, and can choose how to respond.

Will Trance Work for Me? 

Entering a trance state is a natural ability. Some people do it easily and quickly, while others need more time to feel comfortable with the process. It requires no special talent; it's a natural process that your brain knows how to be in.

Will I Lose Control? 

Absolutely not. You are always in control. Trance is deep concentration with heightened internal awareness; you hear every word, remain aware of what's happening, and can choose how to respond at any given moment.

Will I Remember What Happened During the Session? 

That depends on how deep the trance is. In a deep trance, memory may be hazy, and that's exactly what we want, because that's where the most profound transformation happens.

How Long Before I See Results? 

It varies from person to person. Some people feel change as early as the first session, while others start noticing the shift after several sessions. Most people see significant transformation within 5-8 sessions.

Is the Change Permanent? 

Yes, absolutely. The transformation occurs at the neurological and emotional level. When we change patterns, beliefs, and reactions at this level, they remain changed. This isn't temporary or superficial; it's a permanent and long-lasting transformation.

What if Something Bothers Me During the Session? 

It's very important to tell me if something bothers you or doesn't feel right at any moment. As soon as you share it, I'll perform an emotional clearing, and we'll continue, ensuring you always leave the session feeling calm and comfortable.
